### 🔹 Semester 1

#### ⚡ Power Consumption Analysis & Forecasting
*Subject: Introduction to AI & ML*
* **Description:** Developed a machine learning pipeline to analyze and predict electric power consumption patterns. The project evaluates whether energy demand is influenced more heavily by weather conditions or time-based cycles.
* **Highlights:**
    * **Dominant Factors:** Discovered that time-based features (e.g., hour of the day) are the primary drivers of demand, contributing **71.7%** to the model's predictive power.
    * **Weather Impact:** Weather features, such as Temperature and Humidity, account for the remaining **28.3%** of the influence.
    * **Process:** Utilized an **XGBoost Regressor** and implemented automated feature engineering to extract granular data like "day of year" and "week of year" from timestamps.

#### 🎵 Music Track Analytics & Data Exploration
*Subject: Introduction to Data Science*
* **Description:** Conducted extensive Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a dataset of over **8,500 music tracks** to identify key audio characteristics and historical industry trends.
* **Highlights:**
    * **Trend Mapping:** Identified music production cycles and tracked release volume trends spanning several decades.
    * **Data Quality:** Robustly managed a complex dataset with 15 variables, implementing cleaning techniques to handle missing values for genres and artist names.
    * **Process:** Performed statistical profiling using **Pandas** and generated trend visualizations with **Matplotlib** and **Seaborn**.
